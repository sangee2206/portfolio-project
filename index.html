From Cinema to Politics

Vijay,a renowned actor,has stepped into politics to bring transformative change.His journey,from captivating audiences on screen to leading a movement in Tamil Nadu,reflects his deep commitment to public service

Work


Thalapathy Vijay transitioned from a legendary Tamil actor to a full-time politician. His political party, Tamilaga Vettri Kazhagam (TVK), was launched in 2024, with a focus on social justice, secularism, and Tamil nationalism. After decades of cinematic success, he exited films to lead his party and prepare for the 2026 elections in Tamil Nadu and Puducherry. His massive fan following now fuels his political movement.
